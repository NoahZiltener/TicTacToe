Tic Tac Toe

Tic Tac Toe is a classic game played between two players. The game board is a 3x3 grid, and the goal is to get three of your own marks (either X or O) in a row, either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally. Players take turns placing their marks in empty squares. The first player to achieve the goal wins. If all squares are filled and no player has won, the game is a draw.
